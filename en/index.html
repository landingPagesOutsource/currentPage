How Tamika uses Sellzone to get big results for her small business

Step 1

Find winning keywords

Using Sellzone’s Keyword Wizard, Tamika finds relevant and uncommon keywords for her product listings’ “Search terms” field.

Steps to success:

Type

your product name into Keyword Wizard to find hundreds of related keywords

Look

for keywords that indicate uncommon uses of your product, popular misspellings, and synonyms

Add

the keywords to your “Search terms” field, title and bullets. Don’t overstuff your title and bullets with keywords; make them as concise as possible

Find my perfect keywords

Step 2

Learn from
the competition

Tamika uses Sellzone’s Traffic Insights tool to gain insight into shoppers’ interests and to research the keywords her competitors’ listings are ranking for.

Steps to success:

Find

listings relevant to your potential customers by looking at the “People also buy” section in your and competitors’ listings

Enter

these listings into the Traffic Insights tool to see what keywords they’re ranking for

Include

these keywords in your search terms and listings’ content to attract more relevant traffic

Research your competitors

Step 3

Keep your listings updated

Tamika says: “If Amazon says something is optional… it isn’t!” She keeps her listings up-to-date with all the recent Amazon guidelines to ensure top organic performance.

Steps to success:

Type

your ASIN into Sellzone’s Listing Quality Check tool

Check

your listing for incompleteness, Amazon Guidelines fit and content errors, and get recommendations for improvement

Update

the listing to improve your chances of getting a top quality score

Audit your listings
